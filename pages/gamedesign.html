GAME DESIGN

ABOUT

As a game designer, I strive to create memorable and engaging video game experiences that offer unique gameplay. My systems and stories revolve around a single core mechanic, reducing complexity and offering elegant interactivity. Rather than crafting long hours of content, I value multiple replays with surprises and challenges that keep players engaged.

My games are aesthetically nonsensical, featuring bizarre mashups of themes and ideas that I draw from various sources such as everyday life, pop culture, and personal experiences. I believe realism is not necessary for fun gameplay, and that games are imaginary systems built to manifest joy in any time or place.
