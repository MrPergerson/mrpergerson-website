GAME DESIGN BIO

BIO

I'm Cole Pergerson and I am a game designer based in San Jose, CA, USA. Many of my games have been published on Itch.io. Every week I make a new game, which can be found here in this website.

I graduated from San Jose State University with a degree in Digital Media Art, where I was heavily involved in the Game Development Club and ran various events such as the Summer Game Dev Workshop and the Global Game Jam site at SJSU.
